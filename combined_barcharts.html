Country-Specific Overview: Water Usage, Resources, Abstractions & Stress

The charts provide a country-specific overview of water usage, resources, and abstractions across Europe from 1990 to 2022. You can interact with the charts by toggling between different views and hovering over data points to see exact values. In the water usage and abstraction charts, you can hide or show specific categories by toggling the legend items. Additionally, you can switch between a stacked view and a percentage-stacked view for deeper insights and use the "Sort Descending" button to reorder the countries or data categories for easier comparison and analysis.

Another key visualization highlights the Top 5 Countries by Water Stress, showcasing the countries with the highest water stress from 1990 to 2022. This chart provides a clear breakdown of usage relative to abstractions, with interactive tooltips displaying precise values for each country and a dynamic legend that updates based on the selected year.
