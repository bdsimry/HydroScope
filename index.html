HydroScope: A Deep Dive into Europe's Water & Resource Dynamic

Introduction & Background

Water usage and resource management in Europe are critical issues that affect both the environment and the economy. As populations grow and climate change impacts water availability, understanding how water is consumed and distributed across different sectors is crucial for sustainable development.

This project explores water usage patterns across Europe, highlighting the challenges faced by different countries and regions. By examining trends in consumption, availability, and resource management, we aim to provide valuable insights into how Europe can improve its water management practices for the future.

  • Analyze the current state of water usage across Europe.
  • Track changes in water resource availability from 1990 to 2022.
  • Examine sector-specific water consumption patterns, including agriculture, industry, and households.
  • Highlight regions facing the most severe water scarcity challenges.
  • Identify opportunities for improving water management and efficiency across Europe.
